Who Can Vote in India?

In India, every citizen who is 18 years or older on the qualifying date (January 1st of the year) is eligible to vote, provided they are registered on the electoral roll. Voting is a fundamental right and a vital part of the democratic process.

Eligibility Criteria:
✔️ Must be an Indian citizen
✔️ Must be 18 years of age or older
✔️ Must possess a valid Voter ID card
✔️ Must be registered in the electoral roll of the constituency
