About the Online Voting System

The Online Voting System is a secure, transparent, and efficient platform designed to simplify the electoral process in India. It enables registered citizens to vote from anywhere using their Aadhar number as a primary identity verification.

This official portal ensures accessibility, data protection, and fair voting practices using modern technology. Each voter must register with a unique username, valid Aadhar number, and verified phone number.

Your vote is your voice. Let’s build a stronger democracy — one vote at a time!
